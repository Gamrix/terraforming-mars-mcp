## What Went Right

### 1. Milestones: 10 VP — best ever, claimed 2 of 3
Terraformer claimed Gen 7 at TR 36 (sold Mining Rights + Permafrost to fund 8 MC). Gardener claimed Gen 8 after plant conversion gave 3rd greenery. This is the first time claiming 2 milestones. 5 VP advantage over John (his 1 milestone Planner).

### 2. VP-per-resource strategy continues to improve
- Ants: 17 microbes = 8 VP (fed by Extreme-Cold Fungus + stealing from NRB/Decomposers)
- Security Fleet: 7 fighters = 7 VP (fed by Ti production)
- Pets: 11 animals = 5 VP (from 11+ city placements across both players)
- Herbivores: 5 animals = 2 VP (reduced by Predators stealing)
- Total VP-per-resource: 22 VP. Up from 35 card VP in Game 4.

### 3. TR lead: 52 vs 49
Strong terraforming from Great Aquifer (2 oceans early), Terraforming Ganymede (+4 TR), Rad-Chem Factory (+2 TR), multiple heat conversions, standard Asteroids, Regolith Eaters (maxed O2), Imported Hydrogen (final ocean).

### 4. Saturn Systems Jovian synergy worked
SS triggered on every Jovian tag from both players. MC prod rose from initial to 20 by endgame. Io Research Outpost + Asteroid Mining + Terraforming Ganymede gave early Jovian triggers, and John's Vesta Shipyard, BFATA, Miranda Resort, Ganymede Colony also triggered SS for free MC prod.

### 5. Extreme-Cold Fungus + Ants combo was devastating
ECF added 2 microbes to Ants every gen. Over 4 gens: 8 microbes = 4 VP from ECF alone. Combined with stealing from John's microbe cards, Ants became the highest-VP single card at 8 VP.

## What Went Wrong

### 1. AWARDS SWEPT AGAIN: 0 VP vs 15 VP (–15 VP swing)
Funded Miner at 8 MC when leading 16-2 in Gen 4. By endgame, John led 23-17 from massive steel+Ti production (4 steel prod + 6 Ti prod). Never funded another award. John funded Banker (61 vs 20 MC prod) and Thermalist (21 vs 4 heat at end).

**Root cause**: Miner award counts RESOURCE CUBES, not production. Spending all Ti on space cards (Ice Asteroid, Security Fleet, Imported Hydrogen) depleted my stockpile. John's Strip Mine + Titanium Mine + Vesta Shipyard + Mineral Deposit gave him both production and stockpile advantages.

**Key lesson**: Don't spend your Miner-relevant resources freely after funding Miner. Either stockpile for endgame or don't fund it. The 8 MC was wasted AND John got 5 VP from winning MY award.

### 2. CITY ADJACENCY GAP: 9 vs 25 (–16 VP)
Claude had 3 on-Mars cities (46, 35 Capital, 29 Lava Tube) vs John's 7+ on-Mars cities. John placed cities at 55, 41, 48, 50, 57 Commercial District, 58, 19 strategically adjacent to greenery clusters.

**Root cause**: 
1. Only 2 city cards played (Research Outpost, Capital, Lava Tube Settlement — but LTS came too late Gen 13). 
2. Never used Standard City project (25 MC) during mid-game when greenery clusters were forming.
3. John placed cities adjacent to MY greeneries (41 next to 40, 48 next to 47) stealing adjacency VP.

**Key lesson**: Must play 5+ cities. Use SP City aggressively when greenery clusters form. Place cities BEFORE greeneries to control adjacency.

### 3. Economy gap: 20 MC prod vs 61 MC prod
John's economy exploded: Tharsis Republic (+1 per city x 9 cities), Earth Catapult (-2 on all), Satellites (+7 from space tags), Space Hotels (+4), Sponsors (+2), Miranda Resort (+5 from Earth tags), Gene Repair (+2), Food Factory (+4), multiple city MC prods. Total: 61 MC prod.

Claude's economy: Saturn Systems Jovian triggers (total +7 MC prod from all Jovian tags), Capital (+5), Zeppelins (+5), Investment Loan (-1). Total: 20 MC prod.

This 3:1 economy ratio meant John played 3-4x more cards and standard projects per gen. He played 70+ cards vs my ~35.

### 4. Plant production stolen repeatedly
Birds (-2 plant prod), Fish (-1 plant prod), Herbivores initially helped but Predators stole animals every gen. My plant prod went from peak 4 down to 1 by endgame. Couldn't sustain greenery production.

## Key Lessons

1. **City count is the #1 problem.** 3 on-Mars cities is not enough. John's 25 city adjacency VP vs my 9 is a 16 VP gap — larger than the entire awards category. Must build 5+ cities using SP City (25 MC) in mid-game. Each city is worth 3-6 adjacency VP.

2. **Awards strategy needs complete rethink.** 0 awards in 3 consecutive games is unacceptable. Miner looked safe at 16-2 but collapsed when I spent Ti and John built production. Options: (a) fund awards only in the last 2-3 gens when stockpiles are near-final, (b) fund Scientist where tag count is permanent and harder to swing, (c) accept that some awards are unwinnable and focus on denial or alternative VP.

3. **Don't spend Miner resources after funding Miner.** Every Ti spent on space cards reduces Miner score. Security Fleet converts Ti to fighter VP (1:1) but costs a Miner point. If Miner is funded, keep Ti stockpile for endgame.

4. **Protect against plant production theft.** Birds, Fish, Predators all stole production/animals. Need Protected Habitats or accept that animal/plant strategies are vulnerable in 2-player. Diversify VP sources that can't be stolen.

5. **ECF + Ants combo is top-tier.** 2 microbes per gen to Ants = 1 VP per gen. Over 4 gens = 4 VP for essentially free (ECF action cost). Draft Extreme-Cold Fungus whenever available and temperature allows.

6. **Economy must be prioritized earlier.** 20 MC prod vs 61 is unrecoverable. Need to reach 30+ MC prod by Gen 8. Saturn Systems helps but isn't enough alone. Need dedicated MC production cards earlier.
